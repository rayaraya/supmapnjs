Hello!

This is a web application for small-scale traffic modeling.

It allows you to observe traffic in chosen part of the world map. The moving in the definite area depends on traffic congestion and road types. “Clever” cars can “see” others in the determined distances and define their behavior as a result.

For a start select an area by clicking Ctrl + left mouse button. Use “Options” to choose the congestion level and control time boost.
